Who We Are!

ProLaunch Careers is a consultancy dedicated to helping early professionals and job seekers build the skills and confidence they need to succeed in today’s competitive workplace.

We believe that career growth shouldn’t be overwhelming. Whether you’re starting out as a Virtual Assistant, stepping into Customer Support, or preparing for global opportunities, ProLaunch Careers equips you with the training, tools, and guidance to thrive.

“I started ProLaunch Careers after years of working across customer support, operations, and IT. I know the struggles of starting a career with limited guidance, and I want to make that journey easier for others. This isn’t just about skills, it’s about confidence, clarity, and community.”

— Mary-Queen Uchechukwu
